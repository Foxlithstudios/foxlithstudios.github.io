2. Categories of Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Data Collected Automatically (Advertising, Analytics & Network Features)

MindQuiz requires an active network connection to initialize essential game features, manage privacy choices, and serve advertisements. To provide this ad-supported experience, our game integrates third-party Software Development Kits (SDKs). These third-party services automatically collect certain technical and network identifiers:

  • Identifiers Data: Unique device identifiers, advertising IDs (such as Apple's IDFA or Google's GAID/Advertising ID), network IP addresses (processed for country-level and regional location analytics and ad targeting; raw IP addresses are not permanently stored by Foxlith Studios).
  • Device & Technical Data: Device model, operating system type and version, time zone settings, system language, hardware performance specifications (CPU, storage capacity, screen resolution), and network connection type.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Metrics regarding how you interact with advertisements served within the game (e.g., ad impressions, clicks, video completions) used for fraud prevention, frequency capping, and performance measurement.

2.2 Data Stored Locally

Your individual gameplay progress, high scores, completed levels, and round correction statistics are saved locally on your end device using the Unity framework (PlayerPrefs). This operational data is processed locally on your physical device.

2.3 Data You Provide Voluntarily (Customer Support)

If you contact us directly via email or through our in-game support interface for technical support, inquiries, or feedback, we collect your email address, your name (if provided), the content of your message, and any technical attachments or screenshots you choose to send. This data is processed exclusively to handle your request.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party SDK Integrations

We do not sell your personal data. To facilitate essential services, technical execution, and monetization, we securely integrate specific third-party service providers. These companies operate either as independent data controllers or data processors, governed by their own respective privacy disclosures:

  • Unity Technologies (Unity Engine): Used for fundamental rendering, graphic interface mechanics, and basic application framework stability tracking. (Unity Privacy Policy)
  • Advertising Partners (e.g., Google AdMob / Unity Ads): Third-party ad networks programmatically match and serve advertisements over your internet network. These partners access your device's advertising ID to manage ad delivery, prevent fraud, perform analytics, and deliver targeted marketing based on your consent profiles.

5. User Control and Regional Rights

5.1 EEA & United Kingdom Users (GDPR Compliance)

The processing of personalized advertising is based strictly on your explicit consent, which you can grant, deny, or modify at any time via the Consent Management Platform (CMP) accessible in the game's settings menu. If you refuse or withdraw consent, you will continue to see advertisements, but they will be contextual and not matched to your inferred interests.

You maintain the right to access, rectify, or erase any customer support communication logs by contacting privacy@foxlith.com.

5.2 United States Users (CCPA/CPRA Compliance)

Residents of certain US states have the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of their personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. You can restrict programmatic tracking at any time by configuring your global device-level settings (e.g., toggling "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA / Age Restrictions)

Our services are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data, track advertising metrics, or target personalized marketing toward children under the age of 13 (or under 16 within the EEA). If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child in violation of global privacy guidelines, please contact us immediately at privacy@foxlith.com, and we will execute immediate deletion procedures.

7. Data Security and Retention

We apply appropriate, industry-standard administrative, physical, and technical security safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure of data under our control. Customer service emails are kept only for as long as necessary to fully resolve the underlying inquiry. Local gameplay databases can be reset by the user by wiping the application cache in the operating system device parameters or completely uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to revise, update, or modify this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect operational, legal, or technical changes. Important changes will be signaled in the application with an updated "Last Updated" revision date.
